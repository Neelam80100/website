Getting in sync with natural elements, the countryfied design of furniture appears bit worn and informal, being more of casual in looks and cosy in feel. It is less ornate but more functional and serves well as a home's best accessory. The organic appeal of this style makes it more rustic with functional ease.

This is a merger of mission and arts & crafts styles of furniture. In arts & crafts, the prominent features include subtle details and clean lines. The mission style came later with heavier lines and favoured the minimal concept of design. Their combination brought out furniture designs with handcrafted details, rich finishes and use of solid wood to make furniture.

Warm, earthy colours are indicative of a French Country design style, as are worn and ornamental wooden furnishing. The style has an overarching farmhouse inspiration. French Country design may include soft and warm tones of red, yellow or gold and natural materials like stone and brick.
